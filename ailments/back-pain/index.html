About Back Pain

This is a common problem usually felt in the upper or lower back. The pain can vary from feeling moderate to intense. First point of diagnosis would be feeling pain in the back which may be chronic or intermittent.

Symptoms

Pain in the upper or lower back

Causes

  • Back injury
  • Strain on muscles from exercise
  • Incorrect mattress
  • Incorrect posture
  • Work at the desk

Remedies

Accupressure

Chiropractor

Eucalyptus oil (1-2 mixed in oil and then massaged)

Hot Cold Gel pack

Taking Rest to limit straining the back

Correcting posture

Improving working desk environment

Serrapeptase
